About CERN

<>Physicists and engineers at CERN use the world’s largest and
most complex scientific instruments to study the basic constituents
of matter – fundamental particles. Subatomic particles are made to collide
together at close to the speed of light. The process gives us clues about how the
particles interact, and provides insights into the fundamental laws of nature. We want
to advance the boundaries of human knowledge by delving into the smallest building blocks of our universe.
